On Campus Living

Living on campus is a great option! Here is some helpful information:

There are many housing options on campus. For 1st years the options are Redwood Hall, Cypress Hall, or Honors Hall.

  • Redwood Hall- This hall has 6 floors of coed dorms. These dorms are 2 person with communal bathrooms, there are also single dorms.
    [2 person=$4,620 1 person=$5,310]
  • Cypress Hall- This hall has 8 floors. There are suites and there are single rooms (these are harder to get). Each suite has 4 people with 2 rooms and 1 bathroom
    [suite =$4,950 single =$6,010]
  • Honors Hall- This hall is only for honors students (of any year), it is mainly suite style.
    [typically honors students get free housing]
  • Other options- If you are not a first year there are many other options like Greek Village, Oak Hall, Laurel Hall, or Mapel Hall
    • There are many options for meal plans, choosing whats best for you is important!
