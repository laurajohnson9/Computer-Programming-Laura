How to Apply


The Process of Application to NJIT

The steps to apply to NJIT are relatively simple. The university is connected to the Common Application which means, the first step is to create your Common App account. The next step is to select the New Jersey Institute of Technology as one of your schools. It is required to complete the questionnaire, submit letters of recommendation, and high school transcripts. When applying to the Hillier College of Art and Design, it is important to meet the portfolio requirements. NJIT is a test optional university so SAT and ACT scores are not required but can be submitted if preferred. Lastly, the final step is pay the application fee.
