Transportation



Ways To Get To Campus

NJIT is located in University Heights in Newark, NJ. There are multiple ways of transportation to get to campus. The university is a couple blocks away from Newark Penn Station that has trains that travel to multiple parts of the state. The station is only a 20 minute walk from campus or other forms of transportation can be used to get there quicker. NJIT has its own light rail station located on Warren St which is just across the street from the Wellness Center. The lightrail can be used to get around Newark or places close in distance. Another reliable source of transportation is the Rutgers bus or NJ Transit bus. For the Rutgers Bus, it stops directly in front of the NJIT Bookstore and has a set schedule for its stops. The NJIT transit bus also has multiple stops near NJIT which will just leaves one with a walk of only a few minutes.





Costs For Transportation

    -The cost for the NJ Transit train to Newark Penn can vary depending on where you are coming form. For this type of transportation there isn't a specific price.
    -The Newark Light Rail is at a standard price of $1.60.
    -The Rutgers Bus in Newark is fare free but you will need your ID to get on.

Student Tips:

1. The best way to get to your classes on time is by taking the trains or busses before your planned arrival time so if any inconvenience with public transport, you will be still on time to class.
2. It is better to use the Rutgers Bus from Newark Penn Station just to save yourself the light rail fare.
3. Make sure to pay attention to any changes to your source of transportation, and if their times have been delayed.
4. In the first week of classes, try to figure out a system that works best so you can get to your classes on time and don't have a stressful trip to campus.
