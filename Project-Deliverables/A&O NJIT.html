Athletics, Activites and Organizations


NJIT has over 150 student organizations (240 are listed on on the Highlander hub found here! .) These organizations fall under the categories of Civic Engagement/ advocacy, Councils and Committees, Cultural/Spiritual, Departmental, Honors Societies, Professional & Service Fraternities, Professional/Academic, Recreational, Social Fraternities and Sororities, and Special interests. NJIT Fraternity & Sorority Life (FSL) organizations hold history that expands over 100 years. Many have wonderful things to say about their experiences in FSL organizations!

Eligibility Requirements For FSL

  • A NJIT Matriculated Student
  • Completed at least 12 college credit hours
  • A 2.5 Cumulative GPA
