Random Fun Facts/ Information

  • Most residence halls have game rooms, there is also a bowling alley in the Campus Center
  • Mapel Hall has a fire pit aswell as a grill on the 2nd floor outdoors, acessable for all residents
  • Some of the best study spots: 1-Sky Bridge 2-Library Basement 3-CC Rooftop 4-Archi Library
  • Tutoring for math and some other subjects is located in CKB on the groundfloor -make an appointment or walk in!
  • Mail can be picked up at either one of the amazon lockers or in your residence hall office
  • One Amazon locker is in the book-store and the other is in the Campus Center
  • you can get from one side of campus to the other in about 5 mins.
